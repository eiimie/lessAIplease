Less AI, Please!

Filter out AI-generated images to improve the accuracy of your visual references. Whether you're a student, researcher, or artist, this tool will help you find more authentic visual content online.

When enabled, this filter will block AI-generated images from appearing in your search results. You can turn this on or off at any time.

When enabled, this filter will block any result (not just images!) from after the year 2022. You can turn this on or off at any time.

Why use this filter?

  • Find more accurate and authentic visual references
  • Filter out AI-generated content when accuracy is critical

Please note that this extension can result in false positives or false negatives.

Developed with students, academics, and professionals in mind.
